About the band

One Direction, also shortened to 1D, are an English-Irish pop boy band formed in London, England in 2010. The group are composed of Niall Horan, Liam Payne, Harry Styles and Louis Tomlinson; former member Zayn Malik departed from the group in 2015. The group signed with Simon Cowell's record label Syco Records after forming and finishing third in the seventh series of the British televised singing competition The X Factor in 2010. In their definite 5 years of activity, the band released 5 chart-topping albums, a majority of which being recorded while on the road for touring.

The band went on hiatus in January 2016, allowing all members to pursue other projects, promising a swift return in 18 months however as on 2020, they are still separated.

...and their fandom

Though they had not even won their season of The X Factor, the band reached an unprecdented level of fame worldwide thanks to social media. Sprouting from a small fanbase of X Factor viewers, word spread about the band through Twitter and Tumblr like wildfire. The five boys became pop idols at the ages of 16, 17, and 18, causing mass hysteria across the globe, comparable to that of Beatlemania.

Connected by a common love for the band, the 1D fandom grew exponentially. propelling the boys into overnight stardom while simultaneously forming its own community. In it, fan would share news, create fan projects, and essentially create a grassroots promotional campaign for the group that allowed their massive market success.

Throughout the years, the fandom has evolved into its own entity with an extensive, dramatic, and hilarious track record that this website seeks to represent.
